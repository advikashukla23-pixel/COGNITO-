# Cognito

A metacognitive stress-mapping app for GenZ, built on original Shannon entropy research.

## What it does
Cognito asks you to describe what's stressing you, then uses AI to extract your stress domains and calculate Shannon entropy across them — showing whether your stress is compressed onto one axis or distributed across many. Based on your pattern, it gives you a personalized emotional insight and specific weekly tasks to act on.

## Why it exists
Built alongside a research paper on metacognition and Defensive Merger — the phenomenon where multi-axis stress compresses onto a single emotional proxy. Cognito is the real-world application of that framework.

## Built with
React · Claude API (Anthropic) · Shannon entropy · Web Speech API

## The idea
Your stress doesn't live in neat boxes. It bleeds between domains — and the way it bleeds tells you something important.
